Classic Spaghetti Bolognese

A hearty and comforting Italian classic, this Spaghetti Bolognese recipe is rich, flavorful, and perfect for family dinners. It’s simple enough for weeknight cooking but delicious enough to serve at gatherings.

Recipe Information

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Cook Time: 45 minutes

Servings: 4 people

Difficulty: Intermediate

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 can (400g) chopped tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • Freshly grated Parmesan cheese (for serving)

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add chopped onion and sauté until soft, about 5 minutes.
  3. Stir in minced garlic and cook for 1 minute.
  4. Add ground beef and cook until browned, breaking it apart with a spoon.
  5. Mix in tomato paste, chopped tomatoes, oregano, and basil.
  6. Season with salt and pepper. Simmer uncovered for 30 minutes.
  7. Meanwhile, cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  8. Drain spaghetti and toss with the Bolognese sauce.
  9. Serve hot, topped with Parmesan cheese and fresh basil if desired.

Tips & Notes

For a richer flavor, add a splash of red wine to the sauce while simmering. If you like spice, add a pinch of chili flakes. To save time, the sauce can be made ahead and stored in the fridge for up to 3 days.
